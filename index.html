Biography

I'm a PhD student at Universität Stuttgart, advised by Prof. Dr. Michael Pradel. I completed my MSc in 2023 under supervision of Prof. Dr. Marcelo d'Amorim at Federal University of Pernambuco and my BSc. in 2021 under supervision of Prof. Dr. Rohit Gheyi at Federal University of Campina Grande. During my BSc, I conducted research in software testing under supervision of Prof. Dr. Patrícia Machado.

I'm interested in improving software quality to make everything that depends on software better.
